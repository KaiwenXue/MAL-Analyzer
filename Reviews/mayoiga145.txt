      
    The concept behind this anime has so much potential but it falls short. The first episode has all the characters introduced. I imagined this series would have me taking notes but instead I got weak archtypes and interesting characters that are never fully realized.  Some characters are there just so there are enough people to fill the bus.  

The spooky horror aspect of this show first comes across interesting like the way we wondered what was at the bottom of Jon locke's hatch, but eventually everything gets so in depth explained no child watching this show could ever get left behind. Like a

          
        mid battle Naruto speech from one of the bad guys explaining his secret move. 

Good start but they fucked it up.
Watch the first two episodes and then write fan fiction about it. I'd be interested in that and I've never read fan fiction.
